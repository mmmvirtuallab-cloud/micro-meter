Principle

A micrometer works on the screw and nut principle. When you rotate the thimble, the spindle moves forward or backward in a straight line.

Formulae

  • Total Reading = Main Scale Reading + (Thimble Reading *Least Count)
  • Least Count = Pitch of Screw ÷ No. of Divisions on Thimble

Applications

Used for:

  • To measure outside diameter of rods, wires, and bolts.
  • To check thickness of metal sheets or parts.
  • Used in workshops and industries for accurate measurements.
